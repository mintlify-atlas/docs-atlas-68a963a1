## What is Flourishing?

Flourishing is a research implementation exploring the **f-factor**: a general factor of human wellbeing that spans positive emotion, engagement, relationships, meaning, accomplishment, and health (PERMA+H framework).

This project implements four mathematical models from the paper "Toward f: A General Factor of Human Flourishing":

1. **Bifactor CFA** — Confirmatory factor analysis separating general flourishing from domain-specific factors
2. **Mutualism Dynamics** — Coupled logistic growth showing how positive manifolds emerge without latent variables
3. **Lyapunov Stability** — Resilience analysis of wellbeing equilibria
4. **Capabilities-Adjusted f** — Non-compensatory measurement using Nussbaum thresholds

## Analysis Pipeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Data">
    Acquire wellbeing indicators from WHR, OECD BLI, and World Bank APIs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract f-Factor">
    Run PCA to identify the general flourishing factor from country-level data
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fit Models">
    Calibrate bifactor CFA, mutualism dynamics, and stability models
  </Step>
  <Step title="Individual Validation">
    Replicate findings using MIDUS individual-level psychological data
  </Step>
</Steps>
